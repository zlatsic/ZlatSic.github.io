# The AIMM paper

The AIMM component, described in the previous portfolio entry, got additional coverage with a scientific article describing the way it is implemented and integrated into a Hat-based SCADA system. The paper gives a thorough description of its architecture and the integration practices. It is followed up with a concrete case study that uses the component to perform power grid state estimation, which serves as a proof of concept. The paper was published in IEEE Access (opens new window) and it is available through open-access.
